Stage 1: Thoughts
  • Reach out to the customer, pause for 10 minutes.
  • If no response, pause again for another 10 minutes.
  • Continue this process for up to 30 minutes.
Stage 2: Intent
  • When to Pause
  • Reach out and pause for up to 30 minutes.
  • If no reply, expedite and signpost, then pause another 30 minutes.
  • If still no reply, send a second nudge asking the customer to confirm safety, then pause for another 30 minutes.
  • Why Pause: Gives customer space to reply while keeping chat open.
  • When Not to Pause: If no reply after second nudge, call 999.
Stage 3: Means
  • Reach out, pause for 10 minutes.
  • No reply, pause for another 10 minutes.
  • Still no reply, send a message to let the customer know you may call 999 if they don't confirm safety.
  • Pause for another 10 minutes.
  • If no response, call 999.
Stage 4: Timeframe
  • Reach out, pause for 10 minutes.
  • Still no reply, send a message to let the customer know you may call 999 if they don't confirm safety, then pause for 10 minutes.
  • If still no reply, call 999.
